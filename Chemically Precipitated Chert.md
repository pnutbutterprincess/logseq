[[Types of Chemical Sedimentary Rocks]]

Quartz crystals precipitate and replace calcite crystals, also known as *replacement chert* or *nodule chert* because it is formed within other rock and in nodule shapes

Can form in limestone.

Can form petrified wood in silica-rich volcanic ash beds.

Agate is a type of chert that does not replace other minerals, but instead fills hollows in a rock
