- a subphylum of [arthropod](arthropoda)s
- includes the clades
	- ellipura
		- class collembola (springtails)
		- order [[protura]] (coneheads)
	- cercophora
		- class [[insecta]]
		- order diplura (two-pronged bristletails)
- first appeared in the [[Devonian]]
