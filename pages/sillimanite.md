- an aluminosilicate [[nesosilicate]] [[mineral]], polymorph of [[andalusite]] and [[kyanite]]
- chemical formula: [Al](aluminum)2[Si](silicon)[O](oxygen)5
- named after American chemist [[Benjamin Silliman]]
- crystal system: [[orthorhombic]]
- crystal class: [[dipyramidal]] (mmm)
- space group: Pbnm
- color: colorless or white to grey, brown, yellow, yellow-green, grey-green, blue-green, blue
	- colorless in thin section
- streak: white
- crystal habit: prismatic crystals, [[fibrous]], [[acicular]]
- cleavage: {010} perfect
- fracture: splintery
- hardness: 7
- luster: vitreous to subadamantine, silky
