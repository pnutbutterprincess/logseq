- <i>hand hunters</i>
- [[coelurosaur]] [[theropod]]s including birds and non-avian dinosaurs more closely related to birds than Ornithomimids
- First appeared in the [[Middle Jurassic]] and survive today as modern birds
- Description
	- long arms
		- three-fingered hands
		- semi-lunate (half-moon shaped) bone in the wrist (carpus)
	- [[therizinosaur]]s, [[dromaeosaurid]]s, [[aviala]]ns, some [[troodontid]]s
		- a backwards-pointing hip (pubic) bones
