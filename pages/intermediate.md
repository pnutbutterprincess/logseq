- intermediate [[igneous]] rocks are between 55-65% [Si](silicon)[O](oxygen)2
- intermediate rocks include
	- [[diorite]]
	- [[quartz diorite]]
- abundant [[plagioclase]]
- minor or absent [[K-feldspar]] and [[quartz]]
- major [[mafic]] minerals include
	- [[hornblende]]
- may also include
	- [[biotite]]
	- [[pyroxene]]s
