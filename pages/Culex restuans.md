- a species of [mosquito](Culicidae) known to occur in Canada, the United States, Mexico, Guatemala, Honduras, and the Bahamas.
- description
	- a medium-sized brown mosquito, sometimes called the *white-dotted mosquito* due to two white dots sometimes found on the dorsal scutum, but these scales can fall off and are not a reliable distinguishing characteristic
