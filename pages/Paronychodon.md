- a [[theropod]] tooth taxon, a [[form taxon]] that some think may be representative of juvenile [[deinonychosaur]]s
- named by [[Edward Cope]] in [[1876]]
- the type species, *Paronychodon lacustris,* is from the [[Judith River Formation]] in [[Montana]] dating to 75Ma during the [[Campanian]] up to the [[End-Cretaceous mass extinction]]
