- Early [[synapsid]]s in the late [[Carboniferous]] and early [[Permian]], evolving into [[therapsid]]s in the [[Mesozoic]].
  
  Locomotion: strong fore limbs, stiff spine, and light rear limbs providing forward locomotion
  
  Diet: insectivorous, carnivorous, herbivorous
  
  Ex: [[Dimetrodon]]
  Earliest pelycosaur: Archaeothyris
  Additional groups: [[Ophiacodont]]s, [[Sphenacodont]]s, [[Caseid]]s, [[edaphosaur]]s
