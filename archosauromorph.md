Dinosaurs are archosauromorphs

Dinosaurs have a hinge-like ankle joint, while non-dinosaur archosauromorphs have a ball-and-socket ankle joint

Pterosaurs have a hinge-like ankle bone, but because they have so many distinct adaptations for flying, they are not considered part of the dinosaur family tree
